Welcome to Hong Kong!

City information:

Hong Kong is a special administrative region (SAR) of the People's Republic of China. It comprises 200 islands located in Eastern Asia bordering the South China Sea and China. Much of Hong Kong's terrain is hilly to mountainous with steep slopes.

Official languages in Hong Kong:

  • Cantonese (majority people are speaking with)
  • Mandarin
  • English

Hong Kong have three main regions:

  1. Hong Kong Island
  2. Kowloon Peninsula
  3. New Territories
